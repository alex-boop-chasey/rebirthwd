## Why PageSpeed Insights Matters for Bundaberg Businesses in 2026

PageSpeed Insights (PSI) analyses your live website and provides performance scores for both mobile and desktop, based on real-world data from the Chrome User Experience Report (CrUX) combined with lab diagnostics. Google states that sites meeting Core Web Vitals thresholds are more likely to rank well and retain visitors—important when regional internet speeds can vary and every second counts.

For local owners, a quick PSI check reveals:
* How fast your site feels to real customers
* Whether you're meeting Google's current performance standards
* A simple overall score out of 100 for mobile and desktop

Even if you're not ready to make technical changes yet, knowing your baseline score helps you understand where you stand in today's digital landscape.

## How to Get Your Free Performance Snapshot in Minutes

Google designed PageSpeed Insights to be straightforward—no technical expertise required to see your scores.

Follow these easy steps:

1. Open your browser and go to the official tool: **https://pagespeed.web.dev**  
   (Google updated the URL in recent years to this cleaner address while maintaining the same powerful analysis.)

2. Type or paste your full website URL (e.g., https://www.yourbundabergbusiness.com.au) into the search box.

3. Click **Analyze** and wait about 30–60 seconds while Google processes both mobile and desktop data.

4. Review your results:
   * You'll see two prominent scores (0–100) — one for **Mobile** and one for **Desktop**
   * Google colour-codes them: **Green (90–100)** = Good, **Orange (50–89)** = Needs improvement, **Red (0–49)** = Poor
   * Core Web Vitals metrics appear below, showing whether you pass Google's current thresholds

That's it—you now have an accurate, up-to-date snapshot of how Google views your site's performance in 2026.
