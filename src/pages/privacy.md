## Collecting and Using Your Personal Information

### Types of Information Collected

#### Personal Information

We may collect personal information you voluntarily provide, such as:

- Name
- Email address
- Phone number
- Business details (when enquiring about services)

This typically occurs when you fill out a contact form, request a quote, or communicate with us.

#### Usage Data

Usage Data is collected automatically when you visit the Website. It may include:

- Your device's IP address
- Browser type and version
- Pages visited
- Time and date of visit
- Time spent on pages
- Device identifiers

#### Tracking Technologies and Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies (e.g., web beacons) to improve user experience and analyse website performance.

Cookies can be "Session" (deleted when you close your browser) or "Persistent" (remain until expired or deleted).

We use:

- **Essential Cookies** – Required for core website functionality.
- **Analytics Cookies** – Help us understand how visitors use the site (via tools like Google Analytics or Plausible – privacy-friendly options).
- **Functionality Cookies** – Remember your preferences (e.g., language).

You can manage cookies through your browser settings. Disabling them may affect website functionality.
